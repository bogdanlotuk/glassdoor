        Shane Homes

        5.0

        Intermediate Architectural Technologist

        Calgary

        The Shane Homes Drafting & Design department is seeking an experienced Drafter to join our team as an Intermediate Architectural Technologist. At Shane Homes, we offer a diverse portfolio of customizable high-quality homes that meet the needs of our customers while providing an exceptional experience.

        Why Join our Team?

        At Shane Homes, we pride ourselves on pushing the envelope on whats standard in the home building industry.

        We provide our employees with fulfilling careers and opportunities for advancement within our growing company. Our employees are encouraged to continuously improve their knowledge and skill sets through empowerment in the workplace, while providing additional job training and mentorship.

        We reward our employees for their hard work and dedication towards meeting company goals with our unique profit-sharing program.Our culture is focused on family values, community involvement and continuous innovation within the home building industry.

        Who We Are Looking For:

        If youre an experienced Architectural Technologist who is passionate about design and the home building industry this may be the position for you! Were looking for someone who has experience modifying house plans and making our customers dreams a reality.

        You enjoy working on a broad range of projects and recognize that there are times when you must put your head down to get stuff done. You possess a mix of creative and technical skills and are able to make recommendations to customers to help them achieve their vision. Youre technical, organized and detail oriented.

        What You Will be Responsible For:

        • Working on a full range of product types: single family homes, zero lot lines, duplexes and fee simple row houses
        • Preparing a complete set of working drawings, including plot plans
        • Able to create new complete construction details as requested
        • Review contract documents and customer redlines to make customer changes to standard plans
        • Review & understand Architectural control guidelines
        • Communicate changes and/or modifications effectively to Area Managers
        • Provide complete work order packages to estimators

        In this role we value (Qualifications & Skills):

        • A minimum of 3 years of residential drafting experience in the Canadian construction industry
        • Experience and thorough knowledge of AutoCAD and/or Revit experience is considered an asset
        • A proven track record of innovative interior details and elevation designs
        • Strong communication skills and the ability to work and mentor within a team
        • Strong knowledge in structural design
        • A high attention to detail and thirst for learning new skills
        • A solid understanding of the residential construction processes, building materials, permit processes, etc.
        • A solid understanding of the NBC AE 2019 and current land use bylaws for Calgary and Airdrie
        • A self-starter attitude with a willingness to problem-solve
        • Highly organized, excellent time management skills
        • A champion of culture

        Company Perks

        • Shane Homes offers a competitive total compensation package including an Industry Leading Profit-Sharing program.
        • Comprehensive Group Benefits program, including a Health Spending Account.
        • Continuous training, education, and job skill enhancement
        • Excellent Company Culture
        • Reward and recognition program

        Job Types: Permanent, Full-time

        Salary: From $65,000.00 per year

        Benefits:

        • Dental care
        • Employee assistance program
        • Extended health care
        • Flexible schedule
        • Life insurance
        • On-site parking
        • Paid time off
        • Profit sharing
        • Vision care

        Flexible Language Requirement:

        • French not required

        • 8 hour shift
